关于颀观 :: About Chipgun

【颀观芯片】是一家半导体设计服务公司，致力于为客户提供全流程芯片设计服务。

Chipgun is a fabless semiconductor design company. Services are provided for customers with full cycle silicon development from logic design through physical design to tapeout & production.
